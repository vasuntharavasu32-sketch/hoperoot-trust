Who We Are

Rooted in communities,
growing with purpose

HopeRoot Trust was founded on the belief that every human being deserves access to health, knowledge, and a dignified life. Since 2004, we've worked hand-in-hand with underserved communities across 12 regions.

We are transparent, accountable, and community-led. Every rupee donated is tracked, reported, and published in our annual financial disclosures.
